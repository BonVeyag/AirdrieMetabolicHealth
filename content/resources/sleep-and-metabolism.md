## Metabolic impact

Short and irregular sleep can worsen insulin sensitivity, appetite regulation, and decision-making around food.

## First improvements to test

- Keep a consistent wake time.
- Reduce bright light exposure late at night.
- Avoid heavy meals and alcohol close to bedtime.
- Keep caffeine earlier in the day.

## Track response

Monitor sleep timing, morning energy, cravings, and glycemic trends for two to four weeks.

## Escalate when needed

Persistent insomnia, snoring, or excessive daytime sleepiness should trigger formal clinical evaluation.
