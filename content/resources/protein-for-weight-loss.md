## Why protein is a priority

Adequate protein helps preserve lean mass and improves satiety, making calorie reduction and fasting windows easier to sustain.

## Practical target range

Many adults aiming for fat loss benefit from an individualized target in the range of roughly 1.2 to 1.6 g/kg/day, adjusted for kidney status and clinical context.

## Simple implementation

- Include a protein source at each meal.
- Start meals with protein and vegetables.
- Use planned high-protein options for busy days.

## Common pitfall

Under-eating protein can increase hunger and reduce diet adherence even when motivation is high.
