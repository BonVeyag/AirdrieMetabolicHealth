## Why this matters

Blood pressure often improves when weight, insulin resistance, sleep quality, and movement improve together.

## Lifestyle sequence

1. Improve food quality and reduce highly processed sodium-heavy meals.
2. Build aerobic and resistance training consistency.
3. Reduce alcohol load.
4. Track home blood pressure at consistent times.

## Medication coordination

As lifestyle response improves, medication needs may change. Never adjust medications without clinician guidance.
