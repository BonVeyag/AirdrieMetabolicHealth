## Project question

Can a weekly in-person program achieve meaningful adherence and early metabolic trend improvements in a real-world clinic-adjacent setting?

## Methods (current)

- Single-site prospective cohort
- Exposure to weekly in-person classes
- Follow-up timepoints: baseline, 6 weeks, 12 weeks

## Outcomes tracked

- Attendance consistency across classes
- Self-reported fasting adherence
- Weight and waist trend
- Optional blood pressure and glycemic markers

## Current status

Data collection ongoing; analysis plan under internal review.
