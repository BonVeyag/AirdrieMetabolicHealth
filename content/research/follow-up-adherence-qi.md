## Objective

Increase completed follow-up visits within 90 days after consultation.

## Intervention

- Standardized follow-up booking before clinic exit
- Automated reminder sequence
- Early invitation to classes within first week

## Result summary

Post-intervention cohorts showed improved follow-up completion versus baseline workflow.

## Next cycle

A second cycle is planned to assess equity by language, shift-work schedule, and transportation constraints.
